CCTVNEWS News Reader

Read the latest CCTVNEWS stories using Chrome Extension!

Download

Now you have fast access to the CCTVNEWS website right from your browser. With only a click of our Google Chrome extension, CCTVNEWS News Read, you can browse the latest news in the news list section, and discover the latest big changes that are happening in China.

CCTVNEWS News Read is easy to use. Simply install the extension directly on your Chrome browser and there you will see our signature golden button. Click it to see the news list and receive direct access to the official CCTVNEWS site for more.

CCTVNEWS News Read is an official offering from the English language news channel of China Central Television (CCTV). It doesn't include advertisements or third party services, nor does it collect user data. We believe the best news should be delivered without any prerequisites.
