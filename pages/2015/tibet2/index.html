VOICES OF TIBET

My Tibet

The ancient land of Tibet has witnessed phenomenal changes since its liberation in 1951 and since the founding of the autonomous region in 1965.

The region is steeped in religious symbolism and has endured a turbulent political history. However, today, the Tibet Autonomous Region faces modern-day challenges of development, as people seek to cherish the fruits of growth while preserving their traditional culture.

Chronicling these challenges and changes, the CCTVNEWS multimedia team traveled to Lhasa, the political, economic and cultural center of Tibet, and Shannan, the birthplace of Tibetan ethnicity, to bring you a glimpse at the extraordinary lives of ordinary Tibetans.

Humans  of  Tibet

Be it the rich history and mysticism or its stunning landscape, Tibet continues to inspire people from all walks of life.For some, it is a land of pilgrims and pilgrimages; for others, it’s synonymous with adventure and nature’s magnificence. But for the people of Tibet, this enchanting land is simply their home. These are the Humans of Tibet.
