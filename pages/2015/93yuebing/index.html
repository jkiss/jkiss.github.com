99A

The 99A is a domestically designed tank
that serves as a key battle tank of the
Chinese forces. It features advanced
firepower, mobility and defensive
capabilities.

DF-21D

The DF-21D is a land-based anti-ship
ballistic missile. It covers a large
deterrence area, and is capable of
fast strike and penetrations.

H-6K

The H-6K is an important strike force
that can carry long-range strategic cruise
missiles and air-to-surface missiles.
It is capable of long-distance raids,
wide-ranging patrol and strikes that
can reach outside defense zones.

HQ-9

HQ-9 is China’s third-generation
surface-to-air missile system. It is capable
of countering saturation attacks,
operating under complex electromagnetic
environments, and intercepting multiple
airstrike weapons.

J-15

The J-15 fighter marks the breakthrough
in China’s fixed-wing aircrafts, turning
from land-based to carrier-based. It is
capable of ski-jump take-offs and
arresting gear landings on China’s
first aircraft carrier, Liaoning.

UAV

China’s UAV unit has been developing
rapidly, and provides a new means for
the military to handle diversified tasks,
including reconnaissance, guides
and strikes.
